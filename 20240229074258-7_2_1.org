#+title: 7.2.1
1. An *embedding* of $\mathbf{Proj}$ is an initial monomorphism.
2. A *section* of a category is a morphism $g: X \to Y$ for which there exists a
   left inverse, i.e. a morphism $h: Y \to X$ satisfying $h \circ g =
   \operatorname{Id}_{X}$.
