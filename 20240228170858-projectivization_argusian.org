#+title: 8.4.4
For every vector space $V$ of dimension at least $3$ the projective geometry
$\mathscr{P}(V)$ is arguesian.

/Proof/. Obviously $\mathscr{P}(V)$ [[id:1a1bef2e-7179-4d55-9e04-45ba65ab68c7][embeds as hyperplane]] into $V \sqcup
\mathscr{P}(V)$.
