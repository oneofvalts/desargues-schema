#+title: 8.4.4 (Projectivization is arguesian.)
For every vector space $V$ of dimension at least $3$ the projective geometry
$\mathscr{P}(V)$ is arguesian.

/Proof/. Obviously $\mathscr{P}(V)$ [[id:ff9c1836-994d-4964-8f0a-2cf2cb7c2ca5][embeds as hyperplane]] into $V \sqcup
\mathscr{P}(V)$.
