#+title: 6.5.1 (Homomorphism)
A [[id:6e3d11c6-ca58-4be8-8d67-787d002ecb70][morphism]] $g : G_1 \dashrightarrow G_2$ of projective geometries is called a
*homomorphism* if it satisfies the additional conditions
- (M_4) if $a, b \in \operatorname{Dom}{g}$ and $ga \neq gb$, then $ga \star gb
  \subseteq g^\natural (a \star b)$,
- (M_5) if $a, b \in \operatorname{Dom}{g}$, $a \neq b$ and $ga = gb$, then $(a
  \star b) \cap \operatorname{Ker}{g} \neq \emptyset$.
