#+title: 6.1.4 (Natural and sharp of a partial map)
Let $f : X \dashrightarrow Y$ be any partial map. Then one defines a map
$f^\natural : \mathcal{P}(X) \to \mathcal{P}(Y)$ and a map $f^\sharp :
\mathcal{P} (Y) \to \mathcal{P}(X)$ as follows: $f^\natural (A) \coloneqq f (A \cap
\operatorname{Dom} f) \ \text{and} \ f^\sharp (B) \coloneqq f^{-1} (B) \cup
\operatorname{Ker} f$
