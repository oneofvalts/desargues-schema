#+title: 8.4.3 (Strict subspace-embedding implies arguesian.)
If $G$ admits a strict [[id:1a1bef2e-7179-4d55-9e04-45ba65ab68c7][subspace-embedding]] into some projective geometry
$\tilde{G}$, then $G$ is [[id:5ee7842e-e726-4250-ace4-2e08243fc153][arguesian]].
